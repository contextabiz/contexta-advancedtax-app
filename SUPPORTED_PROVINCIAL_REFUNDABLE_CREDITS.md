# Supported Provincial Refundable Credits

This document lists the provincial refundable and benefit-style credits currently supported in the app.

## Ontario

- `ON479` Ontario fertility treatment tax credit
- `ON479` Ontario seniors' public transit tax credit

## British Columbia

- `BC479` B.C. renter's tax credit
- `BC(S12)` B.C. home renovation tax credit for seniors and persons with disabilities

## Manitoba

- `MB479` personal tax credit
- `MB479` homeowners affordability tax credit
- `MB479` renters affordability tax credit
- `MB479` seniors school tax rebate
- `MB479` primary caregiver tax credit
- `MB479` fertility treatment tax credit

## Nova Scotia

- `NS479` volunteer firefighter / ground search and rescue credit
- `NS479` children's sports and arts tax credit

## New Brunswick

- `NB(S12)` seniors' home renovation tax credit

## Newfoundland and Labrador

- `NL479` other refundable credits input

## Saskatchewan

- `SK479` fertility treatment tax credit

## Prince Edward Island

- `PE428` volunteer firefighter / volunteer search and rescue personnel tax credit

## Notes

- Some credits are calculated automatically from eligible-expense or eligibility inputs.
- Some credits remain worksheet-driven or final-credit inputs where the CRA/provincial schedule is more specialized.
- Province-specific benefit programs that are calculated fully outside the return workflow are not all modelled here.
